Titans

  • Ion

    Ion: Primary Weapon - Splitter Rifle: A versatile energy rifle that can be fired in quick succession or charged for a more powerful shot. Offensive Abilities: Splitter Rifle (Melee): A powerful melee attack with the Splitter Rifle. Laser Shot (Q): Fires a high-damage laser beam. Defensive Ability - Vortex Shield (V): Captures and returns incoming enemy projectiles. Utility Ability - Tripwire (C): Deploys tripwires that explode when enemies pass through them. Titan Ultimate - Laser Core: Unleashes a powerful sustained laser beam.

  • Scorch

    Scorch: Primary Weapon - T-203 Thermite Launcher: Fires thermite projectiles that explode on impact. Offensive Abilities: Incendiary Trap (Q): Deploys a canister that creates a damaging wall of fire. Firewall (Melee): Sweeps a wide arc of fire with a melee attack. Defensive Ability - Flame Shield (V): Creates a protective barrier of fire. Utility Ability - Thermite Core: Releases a wave of thermite in all directions as the Titan's ultimate.

  • NorthStar

    NorthStar: Primary Weapon - Plasma Railgun: A charged railgun that deals high damage at long range. Offensive Abilities: Cluster Missile (Q): Fires a missile that releases clusters of explosives. Flight Core: Takes to the skies and launches rockets at enemies. Defensive Ability - Tether Trap (V): Deploys a trap that tethers and damages enemies. Utility Ability - Hover (C): Allows limited hovering in the air.

  • Ronin

    Ronin: Primary Weapon - Leadwall Shotgun: A powerful shotgun with a wide spread. Offensive Abilities: Sword Block (V): Reduces incoming damage by blocking with the sword. Phase Dash (Q): Allows the Titan to quickly dash in any direction. Defensive Ability - Arc Wave (Melee): Releases a wave of energy that damages and slows enemies. Utility Ability - Phase Shift (C): Temporarily phases out of reality to avoid damage. Titan Ultimate - Sword Core: Empowers the melee attacks with a massive energy sword.

  • Tone

    Tone: Primary Weapon - 40mm Tracker Cannon: Fires explosive rounds that can track enemies. Offensive Abilities: Sonar Lock (Q): Reveals enemies in an area and locks onto them for guided missiles. Salvo Core: Unleashes a barrage of guided rockets. Defensive Ability - Particle Wall (V): Deploys a barrier that blocks enemy fire. Utility Ability - Radar Pulse (C): Sends out a pulse that reveals nearby enemies.

  • Legion

    Legion: Primary Weapon - Predator Cannon: A minigun with high damage output and a long spin-up time. Offensive Abilities: Power Shot (Q): Fires a single high-damage shot. Smart Core: Automatically locks onto and fires at multiple targets. Defensive Ability - Gun Shield (V): Deploys a protective shield in front of the Titan. Utility Ability - Mode Switch (C): Switches between close-range and long-range firing modes.

  • Monarch

    Monarch: Primary Weapon - XO-16 Chaingun: A powerful and rapid-firing energy weapon. Upgrade Core: Monarch evolves by dealing and taking damage, gaining upgrades to enhance its abilities. Offensive Abilities: Rocket Salvo (Q): Fires a barrage of unguided rockets. Energy Siphon (Melee): Drains energy from enemy Titans, dealing damage and providing shields. Defensive Ability - Energy Transfer (V): Transfers shields to friendly Titans, offering support in team engagements. Utility Ability - Rearm (C): Allows instant reloads, ability refresh, and XO-16 Chaingun recharge. Titan Upgrades: Choose from three Titan upgrades to enhance performance over time. Titan Kits: Customizable kits further enhance Monarch's abilities for strategic gameplay.
